ANTIVIRUS

Antivirus software is a type of program that protects a computer from viruses by scanning, detecting, and removing them. It can also protect against other types of malware, such as spyware. Antivirus software can be pre-installed on a computer, or it can be obtained as freeware or a subscription service.

Here are some examples of antivirus software:

1. Microsoft Defender
2. Norton 360
3. Bitdefender Antivirus
4. Malwarebytes
5. McAfee Total Protection
6. ESET NOD32 Antivirus
7. Avast One Basic
8. AVG AntiVirus Free

Antivirus software runs in the background and provides real-time protection against viruses. It scans files and incoming emails for viruses and deletes anything malicious.
