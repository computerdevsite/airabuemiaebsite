SPAM

Spam is unwanted, unsolicited messages that can appear on a computer in various forms, such as email, blogs, and wikis. Spam is typically sent for commercial purposes and can be sent in large volumes by botnets, which are networks of infected computers

Spam can be a nuisance and can potentially overwhelm email systems, leading to clogged inboxes and slow networks. It can also pose a security risk as it may contain malicious links that can exploit systems or gather personal information.

Here are some ways to reduce spam:

1. Be careful about releasing your email address
2. Check privacy policies
3. Be aware of options selected by default
4. Use filters or spam tagging
5. Report messages as spam
6. Don't follow links in spam messages
7. Disable the automatic downloading of graphics in HTML mail
